About FMAR

Championing the Interests of Rajasthan's Mining Community

The Federation of Mining Associations of Rajasthan (FMAR) stands as the premier body representing the mining industry across the state. For decades, we have worked tirelessly to advocate for fair policies, sustainable practices, and the well-being of mining professionals.

Our federation brings together diverse mining associations under one banner, creating a unified voice that resonates with government bodies, regulatory authorities, and industry stakeholders at both state and national levels.

Policy Advocacy
Representing mining interests at state & national level
Sustainability
Promoting responsible & eco-friendly mining

A Legacy of Leadership

From marble quarries of Makrana to the zinc mines of Zawar, FMAR represents the backbone of Rajasthan's rich mineral heritage — fostering growth, safety, and innovation.
